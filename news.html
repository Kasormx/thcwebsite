¿What benefits do I get from being part of The House Collective?

-Distribution of your releases on all digital platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer, YouTube Music, YouTube, iTunes, etc.

-Custom beats of any style for your launches with THC

-Unlimited studio recordings, in professional studio with advice and I work together with our producers.

-Complete productions (Vocal Mixing, Mix & Master).

-Advertising of all your releases on social networks and our lists of

-Planning, advice and coordination of your artistic project with all your releases with The House Collective.

-Multimedia content campaigns for social networks (Tik Tok, Instagram Reels, Facebook Reels, YouTube Shorts).

-Live presentations to project your music in our events that we will be organizing on a recurring basis.



¿How can I apply to be part of The House Collective?

You will have to send us to our email thcrecs@gmail.com a song of yours, it can be a finished song or a demo, what is important is that the song has to be recorded in the studio (recorded songs will not be allowed on mobile devices).

This is an example of how the mail should be written.



In the writing of the mail the artist will have to write his artistic name, email, and the link of the song (priority will be given to YouTube links, Spotify and SoundCloud) once the artist sends the demo, in a lapse no more than 72 hours The House Collective will contact the artist to making known if he is a candidate to work with the record label or not.

In case of being accepted the artist will have to attend a meeting in the facilities of The House Collective, to talk about the work plan and in case the artist is convinced to sign the contract.







*All these benefits will be obtained by working with us covering a membership of $300 MXN weekly or $1,200 MXN monthly*

*The artist will have to sign a contract with The House Collective in which he will be will stipulate all the clauses of work of the artist with the record label (the contract has no legal validity, in case the artist breaches one of the clauses will not proceed legally, it is a contract of commitment of work with the record label, at the moment in which the artist fails to comply with some of the clauses of the record label will stop working with the artist)*

*The clauses of the contract will not be very demanding, example "The artist to the working with The House Collective commits to releasing at least 1 song every 2 months and a video for social networks on a monthly basis"*

*All your launches will be worked on in our facilities (the location of our facilities will be provided to the artists upon signing the contract and cover the first membership) and the artist will be able to visit the facilities when wishes, even if the artist has no pending releases, the use of the installations is one more benefit for artists*
